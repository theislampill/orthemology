## Problem

The R7 daee application (Decision 0021) modelled a single noetic case well but
treated **meta-noetic memetics** as one paragraph and one crosswalk row
("versioning + transport + revision"). daee itself describes far more **[direct:
README.md; pattern-profiling.md]**: governing epistemic rules that **form,
stabilize, mutate, reproduce, and propagate** through language, identity, and
institutions; loaded labels as compressed carriers; proof-packet reconstruction;
mutation after challenge; recurrence and collapse-radius; and a route-gradient /
field layer. The application also lacked a bearer for a **represented standard**
that persists across episodes and actors, and it never adjudicated whether the
daee "gradient/descent" vocabulary is literal.

## Decision

### 1. A third metaortheme bearer: the represented standard

```math
\mu \ \neq\ \tilde\mu_{\alpha,t} \ \neq\ \bar\mu_{e,j} \ \neq\ \operatorname{Exec}(e, \bar\mu_{e,j})
```

- $\mu$ — the repeatable **metaortheme type** (normative standard);
- $\tilde\mu_{\alpha,t}$ — actor/institution $\alpha$'s **represented, adopted,
  or embodied** version of a governing standard at time $t$ **[crosswalk]**;
- $\bar\mu_{e,j}$ — the episode-local **metaorthemma** (case-bound token);
- $\operatorname{Exec}$ — the **execution** trace.

A metaorthemma is episode-local; a represented standard **persists across
episodes and is transmitted among actors**. Without $\tilde\mu$, "a standard
propagates / is inherited / mutates" is forced onto the wrong object class.
$\tilde\mu$ is an application-level bearer, **not** a new core primitive and
**not** the actor's whole noetic profile.

### 2. Carrier analysis (one artifact, several roles)

A slogan, proof packet, passage, or institutional norm may simultaneously be a
concrete **carrier token** (orthemma), bear semantic **orthemes**, **carry** a
represented standard $\tilde\mu$, **instantiate** a metaortheme $\mu$, be used as
a case-bound **metaorthemma** $\bar\mu$, and figure in an episode **execution**.
These are roles under different analyses, never one object.

### 3. A metaorthemic ecology (three distinct histories)

```math
\Gamma^{\mu}_t = (V^{\mu}_t, E^{\mu}_t)
```

nodes: actors, institutions, artifacts, represented standards $\tilde\mu$,
metaortheme types $\mu$; typed edges: transmission, citation/inheritance,
compression, mutation, reinforcement, rebuttal, replacement, episode-application.
The crucial structure it exposes:

> the history of the metaortheme **type** $\mu$, the history of its **represented
> versions** $\tilde\mu$, and the history of its **case-bound tokens** $\bar\mu$
> are three different histories.

$\Gamma^{\mu}$ is an application-level extension; it does **not** become a school-
neutral primitive (the anti-vacuity test does not require one).

### 4. Sound corrective dynamics — G1, not G2

daee's own formalism bounds its gradient/field vocabulary: $\nabla$ is a
"Route-ranking functional … **Not literal physical gradient**" and $\nabla\cdot$
/ $\nabla\times$ are "**not literal divergence/curl unless a rigorous target
space is later defined**" **[direct:
docs/algebraic-notation-and-noetic-formalism.md]**. We therefore adjudicate:

- **G0 (metaphor)** — "gradient/field/attractor/descent" as analogy only;
- **G1 (order-theoretic / route-ranked corrective descent)** — **the adopted
  reading.** A vector/partially-ordered burden state ranked by a governing
  functional, where a step is sound only if it improves a declared ordering,
  preserves hard constraints, hides no burden, retains provenance/uncertainty,
  and triggers a whole-state reread;
- **G2 (literal differentiable gradient flow)** — requires a state space/manifold,
  a scalar potential or typed vector objective, a metric, differentiability, a
  gradient, a step rule, and convergence/stopping treatment; **not supplied**, so
  G2 is conditional/future.

Preferred phrasing until G2's conditions are met: **governed, route-ranked,
descent-like restorative dynamics** — not literal mathematical gradient descent.

**Anti-gaming burden functional.** Raw burden count is **not** a sound potential:
discovery can raise the ledger while improving the epistemic position (fixture
N18). Progress is lexicographic:

```math
\text{disclosure} \succ \text{evidence integrity} \succ \text{dependency correctness} \succ \text{hard constraints} \succ \text{justified holds} \succ \text{legitimate reduction} \succ \text{truthful closure}
```

so the system cannot simulate descent by deleting or concealing burdens.

**Five state spaces stay separate:** actual noetic condition; inferred profile
$\hat p$; runtime/control state; social/metaorthemic propagation field
$\Gamma^{\mu}$; released linguistic action — with actual uptake distinct from all.
Runtime closure $\neq$ demonstrated interior restoration (N19).

**Fiṭrah boundary [creed-internal].** Within the creed-internal model fiṭrah is a
normative orientation / proper-function ground. It is **not** one coordinate, one
metaortheme, one algorithm, or one measured scalar target; any "minimum-entropy
attractor" language is provisional and creed-internal. daee itself states the
Shannon analogy does not prove noetic collapse **[direct]**.
