## The five bearers

```math
m_{\text{disc}} \neq m_{\text{subj}} \neq m_{\text{run}} \neq m_{\text{resp}} \neq m_{\text{uptake}}
```

- $m_{\text{disc}}$ — the concrete objection / utterance / document (**observed**);
- $m_{\text{subj}}$ — the person-at-time / noetic bearer, in scope only when
  legitimately targeted, and **not directly observed**;

## Discourse is evidence about a subject, never the subject

```math
\operatorname{ObservedFrom}(h, m_{\text{disc}}) \qquad \operatorname{About}_A(h, m_{\text{subj}})
```

deformation state-type, if placed at all, belongs to $m_{\text{subj}}$ under a
declared analysis — **never** to $m_{\text{disc}}$ because the utterance happens
to be observed. This is fixture NT3's forbidden move.
