# Epistemological and metaphysical placement of the daee application

Bounded note (Decision 0021 §F). Cross-references the school-neutral companion
(`companion/orthability-and-the-ground-of-intelligibility.md`) and the Atharī
companion; adds no new theological claim.

## F1. Epistemological result — a worked concretization

The application **operationalizes**, in a real designed system, distinctions
the companion develops abstractly:

- **first-order vs higher-order orthing** — assessing a proposition vs
  assessing the criterion that governs the assessment;
- **concrete vs sound reason** — an instantiated judgment token vs its
  bearer-appropriate normative status (Decision 0009);
- **proper-function / warrant architecture** — reliability, configured
  procedure, faithful execution, and truth-linkage as separable channels;
  "proper-function" is a modern comparative reconstruction here, not an
  unqualified classical primary-text claim;
- **source / testimony / perception / inference ordering** — distinct
  evidence/warrant channels, not one scalar;
- **evaluator symmetry** — the practitioner is audited by the same
  architecture (blocks privileged self-certification; does not prove
  non-circularity);
- **machine source-dependence analysis** — the machine may detect dependence,
  support source independence, or hold origin analysis underdetermined; none is
  a `tawātur` warrant verdict (fixture N6);
- **creed-internal `tawātur` warrant** — a separate, school-labeled qualitative
  assessment of actual source units, origin/common cause/copying, path
  independence, non-collusion, transmitter trust and competence, indicants,
  coherence, lineage, defeaters, subject conditions, assessor, and evidence;
  count, popularity, graph degree, and institutional persistence do not suffice;
- **candidate profiles under underdetermination** — several live noetic
  profiles where evidence is thin (fixtures N1/N10);
- **restoration is teleological and analysis-relative**, not a neutral
  classifier output.

The companion already develops this conceptually; the application supplies the
precise crosswalk that was missing.

## F2. Metaphysical result — a worked explanandum, not a proof

daee embodies a **normative, truth-directed noetic ontology**: it distinguishes
proper/improper operation, sound/unsound reason, warranted/unwarranted source
order, and restored/deformed cognition. That makes it a strong **worked
explanandum** for the orthability / proper-function program — *here is an
actual system whose entire operation presupposes such distinctions.*

It does **not** establish the ultimate ground of those standards. It does not
prove:

- the metaphysical bridge (L→O, the companion's §8 boundary);
- irreducible teleology;
- a Necessary Being;
- divine attributes or revelation;
- the truth of its own creed-internal noetic taxonomy.

It **presupposes** a normative and theological analysis and operationalizes
it. The legitimate claim is exactly: *daee is a constructive, domain-specific
realization of noetic orthing under explicit Atharī/Taymiyyan commitments, and
a stress test for Orthemology's ability to keep state, observation, governing
standard, application token, execution, closure, and restoration distinct.*

## F3. Theological boundary

- The application is explicitly **Atharī/Taymiyyan** and **creed-internal**;
  it is not a school-neutral conclusion and not the position of every Islamic
  school.
- `fiṭrah` is a normative disposition, **not** reduced to one metaortheme;
  its school-internal representation is qualitative, defeasible, and
  multidimensional, not a scalar, coordinate, algorithm, discourse-readable
  soul state, or guaranteed attractor. `ʿAql ṣarīḥ` is **not** one algorithm.
- Deformation placements are **not** automatic moral verdicts; motive,
  culpability, and soul-state are explicit non-claims.
- Revelation and source order are **analysis commitments here**, not
  school-neutral results.
- daee does **not** independently prove divine Knowledge, Will, Power, Wisdom,
  or Speech; the reason/revelation firewall (Decision 0010; companion §8)
  stands.

## F4. Main-thesis consequence

The main manuscript identifies daee as a **domain-specific application / stress
test**, never as evidence, and **not** as part of the novelty proof (which
remains an integration proposal adjudicated only by the unrun benchmarks).
