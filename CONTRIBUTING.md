# Contributing

This is a research-stage repository with strict honesty discipline. Contributions must preserve it.

## Claim levels

Five kinds of claim stay separate everywhere: conceptual framework, formal definition, empirical hypothesis, metaphysical thesis, design vocabulary. A change may not silently promote a claim between levels. Deterministic validators/fixtures show *consistency*, never empirical utility; do not word anything to suggest otherwise.

## Citation requirements

Every load-bearing related-work or scholarly claim needs a row in the current sourcing surface for its claim area (start at `docs/sourcing/CURRENT-SOURCING-LEDGER.md`, Decision 0019) with a source, stable identifier, and honest verification status (`WEB-VERIFIED` / `RECORD-CONFIRMED` / `VIA-COMPILATION` / `UNVERIFIED`). Do not manufacture citations for fields the text merely names; weaken the claim instead. CI checks ledger completeness (field presence), never live URLs.

## No empirical overclaiming

Nothing in this repository reports a run experiment. The three designed studies (manuscript §13) and the terminology packets are READY TO RUN, NOT RUN. Words like "validated", "shown", "demonstrated" are reserved for deterministic consistency results and must say so.

## Decision-record policy

Substantive changes to theory, notation, verdict semantics, or program structure require a numbered record in `docs/decisions/` and must never rewrite historical records, archived patches, ledgers, or frozen packets (`archive/`, `docs/provenance/document-history.md`, the terminology v0 spec, `terminology/pilot0/` post-freeze). Supersede; do not edit history. Registries (`docs/verdict-registry.yaml`, `docs/notation-registry.yaml`) drive validation — change them only together with a decision record.

## Terminology status

Coined terms are candidates gated on the unrun benchmark; contributions must not "adopt" a term, and every distinction must remain expressible in ordinary words.

## Theological school flags

Creed-internal material is explicitly school-labeled (currently: Atharī route-specific). Contributions may not present school-internal claims as neutral conclusions, may not collapse the reason/revelation labels, and must represent contrasting schools accurately and neutrally or not at all.

## Privacy and provenance

Never commit: raw transcripts, session identifiers, absolute local paths, private screenshots, credentials, or the private casebook/longitudinal records. The public manifest (`docs/provenance/RELEASE-MANIFEST.sha256`) is regenerated by `scripts/make_manifest.py` with every change; CI enforces it. Model-assistance provenance lives in `docs/provenance/`.

## Mechanics

Run `python scripts/validate_repo.py` plus the validators in `.github/workflows/validate.yml` before pushing. No force-pushes to `main`.
