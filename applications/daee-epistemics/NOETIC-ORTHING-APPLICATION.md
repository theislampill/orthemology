# Noetic orthing: daee-epistemics as a worked application

Every daee citation below is pinned to commit
`c86b3c6673147b8802fe222373a165a37d4d24a8`. Status layers (Decision 0021):
**[direct]** = the pinned daee source explicitly says this; **[crosswalk]** =
this project's typed interpretation; **[creed-internal]** = a claim the daee
analysis presupposes; there are no empirical claims here.

## 1. The engagement as an orthing episode

A daee run intakes a concrete objection/utterance/document, diagnoses it,
routes a response, releases bounded output, and re-reads state **[direct:
README.md; diagnostic-ir.md]**. Typed through Orthemology **[crosswalk]**,
that whole run is an **orthing episode** over an immediate **orthemma** (this
engagement, with identity and version). The public wording/context/source
markers are **observation/evidence** over the orthemma — never the interior
state itself.

## 2. The non-collapsing chain

daee's own **inference boundary [direct: inference-boundary.md]** states the
diagnosed interlocutor field is inferred under uncertainty and that the
language-mediated relation is *not* an isomorphism, surjection, guaranteed
update, soul access, or control of guidance. That maps exactly onto
Orthemology's strongest distinction **[crosswalk]**:

```text
concrete engagement  ≠  observed discourse  ≠  candidate noetic profiles
   ≠  inferred profile (Ψᴵ)  ≠  actual interior condition
   ≠  released response  ≠  actual uptake/restoration
```

`Ψᴵ` is an **inferred candidate profile**, never `O*(m; A)` by assertion and
never soul access. A single discourse event is consistent with several
underlying conditions — sincere confusion, an inherited premise, a malformed
criterion, incomplete evidence, affective injury, identity-protective
pressure, volitional resistance, a mixture, or no deeper deformation at all —
so the surface is an **observation**, and thin evidence licenses a **hold**,
not a moralized placement (fixture N1; N10 is the negative control).

## 3. Deformations are candidate orthemes; the rules over them are metaorthemic

daee names deformation state-types (`hawā`, `taqlīd`, `ʿāda`, `gharaḍ`,
`shubhah`, …) **[direct: seven-deformations.md; modes-of-concealment.md]**.
Typed through a **declared noetic analysis** **[crosswalk]**:

- **"`hawā` is operative in this case"** is a **placement claim** under a
  repeatable noetic state-type — an **ortheme-level** claim requiring evidence;
- the rule distinguishing bias from evidential disagreement, incomplete
  evidence, or sincere confusion, and fixing what evidence licenses that
  placement, is **metaorthemic**;
- this case's concrete evidence, threshold, source status, module version, and
  scope form one or more **metaorthemmata**;
- the whole intake → diagnosis → route → response → reread is the **episode**.

The taxonomy's truth is **[creed-internal]** and is **not** imported into the
school-neutral core (Decision 0021 §4). Placements assert nothing about
motive, culpability, or soul-state.

## 4. Diagnostic IR is a composite control representation, not a metaorthemma

daee's Diagnostic IR carries claims, criteria, grounding relations, governing
epistemic rules, testimonial posture, interpretive filters, routes, and
restoration targets **[direct: diagnostic-ir.md]**. Typed **[crosswalk]**, it
is the **episode-internal control representation** — observations, candidate
placements, governing bindings, route state, and residual/claim ledgers — a
**composite**, **not** one metaorthemma and **not** ground truth. Collapsing
the IR into a single metaorthemma, or into the interior state, is the error
fixture-Review-A attacks.

## 5. Meta-noetic memetics is a longitudinal metaortheme/revision extension

daee describes how governing epistemic rules form, stabilize, mutate,
reproduce, and propagate through language and social structure **[direct:
README.md; pattern-profiling.md]**. Typed **[crosswalk]**, this is Orthemology
metaortheme **versioning + cross-episode transport + recurrence-triggered
revision + impact-scoped reopening (collapse radius)** — a **domain-specific
extension across episodes and actors**, requiring **no new primitive**.

## 6. Two-way stress test: what daee requires of Orthemology, and exposes

The relation runs both ways. daee's operation demands, and Orthemology
supplies, exactly these separations — each a fixture (`N1`–`N10`):

- do not infer an interior state directly from surface discourse (N1, N10);
- preserve multiple live profiles where evidence is thin (N1);
- keep a sound governing standard separate from a defective case binding
  (N3), and token adequacy separate from executor fidelity (N4);
- audit the practitioner by the same architecture — evaluator symmetry blocks
  privileged self-certification without proving non-circularity (N5);
- distinguish source **convergence** from repeated dependence on one upstream
  source — count is not `tawātur`-like independence (N6);
- keep correct **diagnosis** separate from correct **restorative route** (N7);
- block closure while a load-bearing noetic dependency remains live —
  whole-state reread (N8);
- separate runtime closure from actual human uptake/restoration (N9).

Each fixture asserts **no soul access** and admits underdetermination. Their
role is to demonstrate **representability** and to **expose omissions**, never
to validate daee or Orthemology.
