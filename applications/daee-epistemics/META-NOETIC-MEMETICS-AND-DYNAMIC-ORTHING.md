## 1. Three metaortheme bearers, not one

```math
\mu \neq \tilde\mu_{\alpha,t} \neq \bar\mu_{e,j} \neq \operatorname{Exec}(e, \bar\mu_{e,j})
```

- $\mu$ — the repeatable **metaortheme type** (normative standard);
- $\tilde\mu_{\alpha,t}$ — actor/institution $\alpha$'s **represented / adopted /
  embodied** version of a governing standard at time $t$;
- $\bar\mu_{e,j}$ — the episode-local **metaorthemma** (case binding);
- $\operatorname{Exec}$ — the **execution** trace.

A metaorthemma is episode-local; a represented standard **persists across
episodes and is transmitted among actors**. Without $\tilde\mu$, "a governing
standard propagates / is inherited / mutates" is forced onto the wrong object
class. $\tilde\mu$ is neither the normative type, nor a case binding, nor the
actor's whole noetic profile (fixture N11).

## 2. Carrier and carried standard

A slogan, proof packet, passage, or institutional norm can, under different
analyses, be all of:

```math
\underbrace{\text{carrier token}}_{\text{orthemma}}, \ \underbrace{\text{semantic}}_{\text{orthemes}}, \ \underbrace{\text{carries } \tilde\mu}_{\text{represented standard}}, \ \underbrace{\text{instantiates } \mu}_{\text{metaortheme}}, \ \underbrace{\text{used as } \bar\mu}_{\text{metaorthemma}}, \ \underbrace{\text{deployed}}_{\text{execution}}
```

Two failures this separates: a slogan's **wording mutates while the carried rule
is stable** (N12), and **identical wording carries a changed rule** (N13).

## 3. A metaorthemic ecology — three different histories

```math
\Gamma^{\mu}_t = (V^{\mu}_t, E^{\mu}_t)
```

nodes $V^\mu$: actors, institutions, artifacts, represented standards
$\tilde\mu$, metaortheme types $\mu$. Typed edges $E^\mu$: transmission,
citation/inheritance, compression (into labels), mutation, reinforcement,
rebuttal, replacement, episode-application. The structure it exposes:

> the history of the type $\mu$, the history of its represented versions
> $\tilde\mu$, and the history of its case-bound tokens $\bar\mu$ are **three
> different histories**.

$\Gamma^\mu$ is an application-level extension, **not** a school-neutral primitive.

## 4. Memetic modes (typed as ecology/carrier operations)

MM1 loaded-label carrier audit; MM2 proof-packet reconstruction; MM3 mutation
after challenge; MM4 source-dependency / collapse-radius mapping; MM5 **false
tawātur / source copying**; MM6 criterion capture; MM7 restoration of a

### False tawātur is the deepest intersection

A hundred apparent reports may be: independent corroboration; copies of one
upstream source; one institutional standard reproduced; mutations of one carrier;
or genuine convergence after independent inquiry. The decisive structure is not
**count** but **origin, independence, dependency, mutation lineage, authority
order** — exactly what $\Gamma^\mu$ encodes (fixture N14). Count is not
tawātur-like independence.

## 5. Boundaries

- Every mode and edge asserts **nothing** about any actor's motive, culpability,
  or soul-state (fixtures N11–N20; each forbids it).
- The ecology models transmission/mutation of **represented standards**, not
  interior states; runtime closure is not demonstrated restoration (N19).
- Institutional reinforcement/hysteresis (N16) is an **ecology-level** property,
  not an inference about any individual's interior.
- This extension validates neither daee nor Orthemology; it demonstrates
  representability and exposes omissions.
