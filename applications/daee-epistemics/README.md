## What this is — and is not

daee is a **constructive, domain-specific realization of noetic orthing under
explicit Atharī/Taymiyyan commitments**: an actual designed runtime whose
operation presupposes real distinctions among sound and unsound cognition,
proper and improper warrant, fitting and unfitting evidential standards,
truthful and false closure, and appropriate and inappropriate restoration.
Its objects can be **typed through** Orthemology, and its application-specific
machinery **exposes** distinctions Orthemology must keep separate.

It is **not**:

- **not identical** to Orthemology (it is a domain-specific application);
- **not independent validation** of Orthemology or its theology — the two
  repositories share an owner and a related model lineage, so apparent
  convergence may reflect **co-development** and is **not novelty evidence**
  (`SOURCE-BOUNDARY.md`);
- **not proof of any metaphysical bridge** — it *presupposes* a normative,
  truth-directed noetic and theological analysis and then operationalizes it
  (`EPISTEMOLOGICAL-AND-METAPHYSICAL-BOUNDARY.md`);
- **not** a source of soul-state, motive, or culpability verdicts — every
  noetic placement is a claim under a declared analysis, subject to evidence,
  underdetermination, evaluator symmetry, and explicit non-claims.

The school-neutral Orthemology core imports **none** of daee's deformation
taxonomy as truth; within this creed-internal application the taxonomy is a
declared-analysis candidate repertoire (Decision 0021 §4).
