# Argument map — orthability to the ground of intelligibility

**Status: normative step map (R3, 2026-07-20; Decision 0010; Task 9 boundary clarified).** Every step of the cross-framework dialectical argument is classified with its extra premises and rival exits exposed. Cross-framework means common-premise accessibility, not a neutral or coequal tribunal; the declared operative noetic frame of the separate creed-internal route is Atharī/Taymiyyan. Sense key: **L** = `Orthable(m; A)` local analysis-relative aptness; **O** = objective conditions of evaluability; **R** = created representation/application. No step is stronger than its classification; nothing later strengthens anything earlier.

## Distinctions kept separate (never merged)

explanatory metalanguage dependence (our account uses intelligible notions) ≠ ontological priority (what grounds what) ≠ necessity (modal status of the ground) ≠ personality/intellect (step 7) ≠ Will (8) ≠ Power (9) ≠ Wisdom (10) ≠ capacity for Speech (§8) ≠ actual divine Speech and its revealed modality (Atharī paper only).

## The reductio's honest scope (companion §5.3)

Decisive against: accounts that model the origin of intelligibility as an intelligible, norm-governed construction (most actual naturalistic construction stories — evolutionary, social-constructivist, conventionalist). **Not** decisive against: brute modal primitivism; Platonism; structural realism; the deflationary metalanguage reply; emergence accounts that refuse the norm-governed-transition model. Against those its force is comparative (explanatory cost), not demonstrative.

## Slide guard

The forbidden move: from "`Orthable(m; A)` holds relative to declared standards" (L) to "there is a practice-independent global object, Orthability" to "that object requires a personal Necessary Ground." The chain never makes it: step 2's bridge runs through the *truth* of evaluations; step 5 concludes to underived *conditions*, not an entity; step 7's personality claim is a separately-premised conditional. `validate_type_token_semantics.py` checks that companion prose using orthability intensively carries the sense apparatus.
